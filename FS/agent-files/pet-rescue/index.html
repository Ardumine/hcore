🏡 About Us

Paws & Claws Rescue Center is a non-profit organization founded in 2018. We rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned and surrendered pets in the greater metropolitan area. Our facility houses up to 60 animals at a time — dogs, cats, rabbits, and small critters.

Every animal receives full veterinary care, vaccinations, spay/neuter, and lots of love while they wait for their forever family. We've successfully placed over 1,200 pets into loving homes!
